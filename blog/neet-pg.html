    High-Yield Subjects and Topic Weightage for NEET-PG 2025

    Grasping the neet pg 2025 subject-wise weightage is key to a successful prep. The top subjects include Medicine, Surgery, Obstetrics and Gynecology, Pediatrics, and Orthopedics. These carry the most weight in the exam and should be given top priority.

    It's vital to concentrate on the most critical topics within these subjects. This includes Pharmacology, Pathology, and Microbiology. Knowing the topic weightage helps candidates plan their study time better. This way, they can aim for higher scores. The neet pg 2025 subject-wise weightage is divided into several categories:

    • Medicine: 30-40% weightage
    • Surgery: 20-30% weightage
    • Obstetrics and Gynecology: 15-20% weightage
    • Pediatrics: 10-15% weightage
    • Orthopedics: 5-10% weightage

    By focusing on the neet pg 2025 high-yield subjects and understanding topic weightage, candidates can craft a solid study plan. This boosts their chances of acing the exam. Remember, the subject and topic weightage can change annually. Staying current with the latest info is critical for a thorough prep.
